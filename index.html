Product:

If you have a cleaning job that requires a bit more power than your usual mop, you need not look any further for the perfect tool to complete the job! This 14-in-1 steam cleaning mop gives you all the power you need to disinfect and sterilize, clean doors and windows, clean floorboards, and more! Featuring a long list of accessories noted in the features below, there is no job that withstands the power of the handheld steam cleaner included. Lightweight and simple to use, the steam cleaning mop will quickly become one of your most valuable cleaning tools. Simply detach one nozzle and swap it out for another to complete different jobs around the house. Save yourself the money on expensive cleaning solutions and wipes. Nothing will go uncleaned.

 

Use the handheld steam cleaner for:

 

Disinfection and sterilization:

Install the long spray cup on the short spray nozzle and then point and aim. Press down on the steam button and the high temperature will eradicated bacteria.

Cleaning clothes:

Install the spray cup with hairbrush on the short nozzle and then mount the towel sleeve. Aim the spray cup at the clothes and press down on the steam button and move the hairbrush up and down as well as to the left and to the right to cover the entire area of the clothes.

Cleaning doors and windows

Install the spray cup with hairbrush on the short spray nozzle and then install the spray cup for doors/windows on it. Aim the spray cup at the door or window and press down on the steam button and move the hairbrush up and down as well as to the left and to the right at the same time to cover the entire area of the door or window.

Cleaning floorboards

You can choose either the extended spray cup or the short spray nozzle. Then install the bent spray cup or round spray cup depending on your needs. Aim the spray cup at the floorboard and press down on the steam button to remove the dirt and stains in wall corners and on the floor.
